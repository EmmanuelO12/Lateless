Introduction

Welcome to Lateless ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Lateless mobile application.

Information We Collect

To provide our departure time and navigation services, we collect the following information:

Location Data

  • Current Location: We access your device's location to calculate travel times from your current position to your destinations.
  • Destination Addresses: We store the addresses and locations you enter for your plans.
  • Saved Locations: If you choose to save locations (like "Home" or "Work"), these are stored locally on your device.

Account Information

  • Apple ID: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID user identifier.
  • Name and Email: Only if you choose to share them during Apple Sign-In.

Usage Data

  • Plans and Preferences: Your created plans, prep times, and app preferences.
  • App Interactions: How you interact with features to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate accurate departure times based on your location and destination
  • Provide real-time traffic and transit information
  • Send you timely notifications about when to leave
  • Remember your preferences and saved locations
  • Improve our app and develop new features

Data Storage and Security

Your data stays on your device. We prioritize local storage, meaning your plans, saved locations, and preferences are stored directly on your iPhone. We do not upload your personal data to external servers.

For route calculations, we use Apple's MapKit services, which are subject to Apple's privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple MapKit: For maps, directions, and travel time estimates
  • Apple Sign-In: For secure authentication
  • Apple Push Notifications: To send you departure reminders

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Remove your data by deleting the app or individual plans
  • Control: Manage location permissions in your device settings
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications at any time

Children's Privacy

Lateless is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
